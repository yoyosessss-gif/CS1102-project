Project Background

1. Background

  • The Data Deluge: Legal professionals are drowning in unstructured text (contracts, case laws, regulations).
  • From Keywords to Context: Traditional tools rely on exact word matches; LLMs understand legal intent and complex logic.
  • The Productivity Gap: Demand for legal services is rising, but manual document review remains a bottleneck. LLMs act as a "force multiplier."

2. Key Functions

  • Automated Summarization: Condensing hundreds of pages of trial transcripts or medical records into actionable executive summaries.
  • Semantic Retrieval: Finding relevant precedents by describing a "scenario" rather than searching for specific citations.
  • Automated Drafting: Generating first drafts of standard agreements (NDA, Employment) based on specific deal parameters.
  • Compliance Auditing: Scanning massive document sets to flag clauses that violate new regulations or internal policies.

3. Application Scenarios

  • Law Firms: Speeding up "Discovery" and "Due Diligence" processes to focus on case strategy and client advocacy.
  • In-house Legal Depts: Streamlining contract lifecycle management and managing global regulatory compliance.
  • Judicial/Public Sector: Enhancing access to justice by providing citizens with AI-driven preliminary legal guidance.
  • Risk Management: Analyzing historical litigation data to predict the outcome or cost of potential disputes.
