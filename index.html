Topic: LLM for Legal Applications: Opportunities and Challenges

Group Members:

  • Sin Cheuk Yiu (Yoyo) - 58534786
  • Kwok Yan - 58541292
  • Seah Yan Zhu - 60031361

1. Background (The "Why")

  • The Data Deluge: Legal professionals are drowning in unstructured text (contracts, case laws, regulations).
  • From Keywords to Context: Traditional tools rely on exact word matches; LLMs understand legal intent and complex logic.
  • The Productivity Gap: Demand for legal services is rising, but manual document review remains a bottleneck.

2. Key Functions (The "What")

  • Automated Summarization: Condensing transcripts into executive summaries.
  • Semantic Retrieval: Finding precedents by describing a "scenario".
  • Automated Drafting: Generating first drafts of standard agreements.

3. Application Scenarios (The "Where")

  • Law Firms: Speeding up Discovery and Due Diligence.
  • In-house Legal Depts: Streamlining contract lifecycle management.
  • Judicial Sector: Enhancing access to justice.
