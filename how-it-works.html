Technical Principals: How LLMs Work for Legal Text

Transformer Architecture

Unlike older neural networks, transformer architecture processes all words in a sentence simultaneously. There is a self-attention mechanism to compute the relevance of every other word, in order to understand each specific word in the sequence. Multi head attention is used for running multiple attention heads in parallel, while each of them is learning different types of relationships.

Training Process

    Pre-training: Predict next token on massive text corpus to develop language understanding.

    Fine-tuning: Further train the model on legal texts to specialize for particular applications.

How LLMs Handle Legal Text

    Long context: Legal cases can be 100k to 500k tokens with over a hundred pages, models may miss information from the middle of long documents.

    Legal jargon: Archaic terms, Latin phrases, highly specific definitions.

    Structured reasoning: Legal analysis requires logical frameworks, while standard LLMs give generalize responses.

    Citation patterns: LLMs recognize citation formats but may generate fake citations.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation

It retrieves relevant legal documents from a knowledge base, for example the case law database, before generating the answer to get outputs from verifiable sources. The LLM is instructed to base its answer on the retrieved content, rather than relying on the model’s memory, which may be outdated or inaccurate.
